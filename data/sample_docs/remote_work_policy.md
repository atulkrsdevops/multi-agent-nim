# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote roles are available for positions designated as "remote-eligible".
Remote employees must be available during core hours 10:00-16:00 in their team timezone.
Equipment: laptop provided, one-time $500 home-office stipend, $40/month internet for fully remote staff.
International remote work requires HR and legal review.
